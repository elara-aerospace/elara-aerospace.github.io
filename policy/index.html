Legal

Privacy Policy

A privacy policy is a legal document that discloses how a Elara Aerospace collects, uses, stores, and protects the personal information of its users, sponsors, or members.

Terms of Service

Terms of service (ToS) are legal agreements that outline the rules, responsibilities, and rights of both service providers and users, which must be accepted to use a service or product.

Cookie Policy

A cookie policy is a document that provides detailed information about the cookies used on this website.

Website Security Statement

A website security statement is a formal document that outlines Elara Aerospace's commitment to protecting its website-related information assets, detailing the principles, strategies, and responsibilities for maintaining data confidentiality, integrity, and availability.

Software Security Statement

A software security statement is a formal document that outlines Elara Aerospace's commitment to protecting its software-related information assets, detailing the principles, strategies, and responsibilities for maintaining data confidentiality, integrity, and availability.

Rocket Launch Security Statement

A rocket launch security statement is a formal document that outlines Elara Aerospace's commitment to ensure a safe rocket launch.

GDPR Commitment

A GDPR Commitment Statement is a document that outlines Elara Aerospace's dedication to complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Customer Data Processing Agreement

A Customer Data Processing Agreement (DPA) is a legally binding contract between a data controller (typically the customer) and a data processor (usually a service provider) that outlines the terms and conditions for handling personal data under GDPR.

SLA Policy (GitHub)

An SLA (Service Level Agreement) policy is a formal agreement that defines the expected level of service between Elara Aerosapce and its contributors on GitHub code repositories.

Acceptable Use Policy

An Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) is a document that outlines the rules and guidelines for using Elara Aerospace's technology resources, networks, and systems.

Processor Disclosure

A Processor Disclosure policy outlines the rules and procedures for how a data processor handles and discloses personal data.

Subprocessor Disclosure

A Subprocessor Disclosure policy outlines how an organization (typically a data processor) manages and discloses information about the subprocessors it uses to handle personal data.

API Disclosure

An API Disclosure policy outlines how Elara Aerospace handles and communicates information about its 3rd-party APIs to users, developers, and other stakeholders.

Vulnerability Disclosure Policy

A Vulnerability Disclosure Policy (VDP) outlines how Elara Aerospace handles and responds to reports of security vulnerabilities in its systems or products.

DMCA Policy

A DMCA notice is a legal request sent by a copyright owner to an internet service provider or website operator, demanding the removal of content that infringes their copyright.

Imprint

An imprint (or Impressum) is a legally required statement on websites in Germany and other German-speaking countries that provides essential information about the website operator, such as their name, address, contact details, and any applicable registration or licensing information. This requirement aims to ensure transparency and consumer protection by making it easier for users to identify and contact the business behind the website.
