Hello There! We Are

Elara Aerospace

We are an incorporated nonprofit student club with a focus on building rockets. We see the current state of aerospace destined for disruption and want to shape the future of the industry with innovative concepts. We aim to build the world’s largest reusable Methalox rocket made by students and under 30 years olds that reaches 150 km into orbit. Our initiative plans to break multiple world records and has been featured on various media outlets.

Additive Manufacturing

Reusability

High Launch Cadence
