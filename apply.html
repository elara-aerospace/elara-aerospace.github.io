Who we are

Elara Aerospace is among the pioneers in the aerospace industry with regard to student-driven innovation. Our objective is to accomplish the unprecedented achievement of propelling a rocket to a height of 100 kilometres by utilising bi-liquid rocket engines—a accomplishment that has not been previously achieved by any student organisation. We are motivated by a steadfast dedication to innovation, specifically within the domain of liquid rocket engines, and strive to expedite advancements in the aerospace sector by means of groundbreaking research and development.
