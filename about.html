Elara Aerospace

Welcoming you to Elara Aerospace, our team of ambitious students hailing from various backgrounds brings innovation to the table through diversity. Our unmatched combination of abilities and enthusiasms for aerospace propels us to surpass limitations and reestablish potentialities. Become a part of our vibrant community, where insatiable inquisitiveness and unwavering resolve unite in order to mould the trajectory of aerospace exploration. Explore your role in our dynamic expedition as we seek to surpass limitations and establish fresh territories.

Munich, Germany

Exciting news at Ignition Aerospace as we proudly announce our participation in the prestigious Münchner Management Kolloquium, which took place in Munich on the 5th and 6th of March this year. Recognised as one of Germany’s leading business conferences, the MMK attracted over eighty senior executives from a wide range of sectors, including DAX-listed companies, European conglomerates, strong SMEs and start-ups, all coming together to explore the overarching theme of ‘STRONGER TOGETHER - Networking and Collaborating to Build Resilience’.
